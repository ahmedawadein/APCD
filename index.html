Your registry data is stored in a Google Sheet you own.
Follow the steps below to get online.

Step 1 — Create your Google Sheet

Create a new spreadsheet and add 4 tabs named exactly: Patients, Examinations, Operations, Followup

In the Patients tab, row 1 headers:

PID | DateOfRegistration | Name | NationalID | DOB | Sex | Phone | AlternatePhone | Address | Referring | Others | EntryDate

In the Examinations tab:

ID | PID | DateOfExam | Eye | ProcedureType | Examiner | CorneaOpacity | LocationCornea | Density | CorneaDiamH | CorneaDiamV | CCT | CCTOtherEye | PupilDiam | Synaechia | Location | Angle | AngleDesc | IOP | LensDensity | LensMorphology | CataractLocation | Spherophakia | Lenticonus | PHPV | PHPVType | Subluxation | SubluxationGrade | SubluxationDeg | SubluxationLoc | Fundus | FundusAbnorm | CD | AxialLength | AXLOtherEye | Syndrome | Investigations | Consanguinity | FamilyHistory | FamilyHistoryDesc | PupilBefore | Comments | EntryDate

In the Operations tab:

ID | PID | DateOfOp | Eye | OperationName | IOL | AnteriorCapsule | PosteriorCapsule | CapsuleStain | Viscoelastic | AnteriorVitrectomy | Membranectomy | Iridectomy | IrisInjury | Sphincterinjury | Synechiolysis | IrisReconstruction | IrisManipulations | Hyphaema | DroppedCortex | DroppedIOL | VitreousHaemorrhage | OtherComplications | Biometry | SRKII | Holladay | HofferQ | Kreading | K1 | K2 | AxialLength | IOLType | IOLPlace | IOLPower | TargetRefraction | Orientation | Steroids | Atropine | Route | Medications | Examiner | Duration | OperationNotes | EntryDate

In the Followup tab:

ID | PID | DateOfExam | Eye | VisitType | Examiner | Cornea | CorneaDesc | AC | ACDesc | CCT | Iris | IrisDesc | Pupil | PupilDesc | Lens | LensDesc | LensStatus | Opacification | Fundus | FundusDesc | IOP | CD | VA | Sphere | Cylinder | Axis | Gonioscopy | GonioDesc | Comments | EntryDate

Step 2 — Deploy Apps Script

In your Sheet: Extensions → Apps Script. Replace all code with this, then Deploy → New deployment → Web app → Anyone → Deploy

Step 3 — Connect

Paste your Web App URL here and click Connect.
